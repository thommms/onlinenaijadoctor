The Online Naija Doctor is a platform that provides Individuals access to highly qualified licensed Doctors across all specialties by phone, Mobile app, or computer at their convenience from the comfort of their homes.

It is aimed to provide premium affordable healthcare services, overcome the long hospital waiting hours and the social stigma attached to some certain medical conditions in the hospital and finally to Provide 24/7 first aid support for the Nigerian population.
